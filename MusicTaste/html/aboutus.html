Hello, fellow musician! Since you are here, we assume you want to learn more about this project. We are proud to say that we finally can help other musicians by teaching them the music fundamental by providing them the necessary information.
The idea came from a few high schoolers who wanted everything in one place and started working on it. Since then, for 4 years we hear very positive comments about our whole work. Many people gives us new suggestions, ideas and donate to this project to make it bigger and more efficient.
Thanks to them, we managed to open our phisical shop. The first one is in Varna, Bulgaria. We plan to make many more across the country, and the world, in order to let every one eager to learn, grow, join our community and express theit MusicTaste
